3D Virtual Community World

Enter an immersive Three.js 3D environment where all 15 community resources float as glowing holographic cards in a virtual Harrisonburg. Orbit, zoom, and click to explore every resource in a futuristic digital world.

Resource Command Center

Auto-rotating 3D distribution with bloom rendering

3D·GL

Visualizes how Harrisonburg's community resources are distributed across six service categories. Food and Health show the highest concentration, reflecting the city's prioritization of basic needs and wellness support for residents.

Resource Galaxy

Live force-directed network — drag nodes to explore

Network

Maps the relationships between resource categories and individual organizations as an interactive network. Larger hub nodes represent categories with more resources, and the connecting lines show which organizations belong to each service area.

Community Coverage

Resources vs average quality by category

Radar

Compares two dimensions for each category: the total number of available resources versus their average community quality rating. Categories where count is high but quality lags may indicate a need for better funding or oversight.

Community Flow

Resource engagement flowing from categories

Sankey

Traces how community engagement flows from broad resource categories into specific organizations, with each flow sized by the number of community reviews. Thicker flows indicate organizations generating the most community interaction.

Live Metrics

Real-time simulated community activity stream

LIVE

Simulates real-time community activity across three key indicators: resource page engagement, event registration activity, and overall resource access rates. In a production deployment, this would connect to live analytics data.
